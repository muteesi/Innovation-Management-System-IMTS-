Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the submission deadline?

Navigate to the "Set Timelines" section, enter the target dates and click "Launch Cohort Cycle". The system updates target dates dynamically.

Who do I contact for user management?

All database registers and privilege access delegations are governed by IT Administration. Under security guidelines, Innovation Team members cannot alter roles.
