Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a new innovation idea? expand_more
To submit a new innovation idea, navigate to the "Submit New Idea" page from the sidebar. Fill out all required fields in the four-step form, upload any supporting documents, and click the submit button. You will receive a confirmation email with your submission reference number.
How long does the evaluation process take? expand_more
The evaluation process typically takes 5-7 business days from the submission deadline. You will receive notifications when your idea moves through each stage of the review process. You can track the status of your ideas in the "My Ideas" section.
Can I edit my idea after submission? expand_more
Once submitted, ideas are locked for review to ensure a fair evaluation process. However, you can add comments or additional information through the idea details page. If you need to make significant changes, please contact the Innovation Team directly.
What happens if my idea is approved? expand_more
If your idea is approved, you will be notified and guided through the next stages which may include concept development, experimentation, and deployment. The Innovation Team will work with you to develop a detailed implementation plan.
How do I reset my password? expand_more
To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address and follow the instructions sent to your email. If you continue to have issues, contact IT Support for assistance.
