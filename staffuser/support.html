Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the evaluation process take?

The initial evaluation is typically finalized in 5 to 7 business days from cohort cycle deadline. You will receive an automated email once complete.

Can I update a submitted idea?

To ensure fair review criteria, ideas undergo full locking once submitted. However, you can add comment additions at the Idea Details view.
